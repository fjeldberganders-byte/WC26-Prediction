📜 Rules & Scoring
Full overview of how points are awarded.
⚽ Group Matches (predict result)
Points stack on top of each other — an exact correct group match therefore gives 2 + 1 + 2 = 5 points.
Correct outcome (W / D / L)+2
Correct goal difference (in addition)+1
Correct exact score (in addition)+2
🏆 Knockout Matches (predict result)
The prediction applies to the score after 90 minutes. An exact correct prediction gives 4 + 4 = 8 points.
Correct winner+4
Correct 90-min exact score (in addition)+4
📊 Group Standing (bracket)
Calculated continuously from current group tables (points → goal difference → goals → seeding).
Correct group winner (1st place)+8
Correct placement 2nd–4th (per position)+5
Correct team among the 8 best 3rd-placed teams+3
🎯 Team Level (how far a team advances)
Team in round of 16+4
Team in quarter-finals+8
Team in semi-finals+15
Team in final+25
Correct World Cup winner (bonus)+40
⭐ Player Predictions
Extra categories — top scorer, assist king, best goalkeeper, best young player, and statistics predictions for the whole tournament.
Correct top scorer / assist king / goalkeeper / U21+10
Numeric question within ±10% of the correct value+5
Correct country (yellow/red cards)+5
